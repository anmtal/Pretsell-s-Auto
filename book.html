Good to Know

Booking questions

Is the online estimate my final price?

No — the live estimate is a starting-price guide only. Once we inspect your vehicle, you'll get an accurate, upfront written quote to approve before any work begins. No surprises.

Do you work on all makes and models?

Yes. Our ASE-certified technicians service import and domestic vehicles — cars, SUVs, light trucks, and EV/hybrid.

Can I get same-day service?

Many jobs are finished the same day. Pick your preferred date and time and we'll confirm the soonest slot that works — or call the shop at (905) 637-1122.

What if I'm not sure what's wrong?

No problem. Choose "Diagnostics" and describe the symptoms — noises, warning lights, or how it drives. We'll pinpoint the issue and quote the fix before proceeding.
