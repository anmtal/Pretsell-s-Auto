Always. We inspect your vehicle, then give you a clear written quote covering parts and labour. Nothing gets done until you approve the price — no surprise charges when you pick up.

Qualifying repairs are backed by our 12-month / 20,000 km parts & labour warranty, whichever comes first. If something we covered isn't right, bring it back and we'll make it right.

Yes — our ASE-certified technicians service all makes and models, import and domestic, including hybrid and EV maintenance. Not sure if we cover your vehicle? Give us a call and we'll confirm.
